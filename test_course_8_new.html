Organ Transplantation Ecosystem

30 Hours

1About the Course

This online, accredited short-term course is designed for healthcare professionals and individuals interested in organ donation and transplantation. It provides a structured overview of the organ transplantation ecosystem, enabling participants to gain both conceptual clarity and practical insights into the science, policy, and coordination involved in the field.

Through ten thoughtfully designed modules, learners will explore the entire continuum — from organ failure prevention and donation processes to transplant science, immunology, and ethical-legal frameworks in India.

2Course Objectives

By the end of this course, participants will be able to:
• Understand the fundamentals of organ donation and transplantation
• Describe the legal and ethical framework governing organ donation and transplantation in India
• Explain the concept of brainstem death and its certification process
• Outline the steps involved in deceased organ donation and coordination
• Recognize end-stage organ failure and organ-specific clinical considerations
• Appreciate strategies for the prevention of organ failure
• Comprehend the basics of transplant immunology
• Learn the principles of immunosuppression and rejection management
• Explore the emerging role of cell-free DNA (cfDNA) in transplant monitoring
• Explore the emerging role of AI in Healthcare & in Organ Transplantation like Kidney Transplantation
